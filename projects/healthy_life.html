This game is about following a varied and healthy diet with the aim of not becoming anorexic or obese. On the screen, different types of food will be falling down, when Bill collides with one of these foods, he will get fatter or thinner, depending on the calories that the food has.
(table)
You'll start with Bill having 18.5 BMI (Body Mass Index) which is the average figure

Bill is the main character, which you control.

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a mathematical calculation involving height and weight, irrespective of family history, gender, age or race. BMI is calculated by dividing a person's body weight in kilograms by their height in squared meters:
