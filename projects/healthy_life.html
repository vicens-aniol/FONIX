This game is about following a varied and healthy diet with the aim of not being anorexic or obese. On the screen, food will be going down, when Bill collides with one of these foods, it will increase or decrease, depending on the calories that this food has.

You'll start with 18.5 in Bill's BMI (Body Mass Index) that is the common and normal BIM

Bill is the main character, which you control.

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a mathematical calculation involving height and weight, irrespective of family history, gender, age or race. BMI is calculated by dividing a person's body weight in kilograms by their height in meters squared:
